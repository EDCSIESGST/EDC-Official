Get to know us

The EDC Community is a place where the members significantly inclined towards entrepreneurship are lent a helping hand to help them take their entrepreneurial knowledge and understanding to a deeper level. A number of sessions for the same are conducted by trained individuals to enhance the members’ proficiency.

Why Join Us

Under the EDC Community initiative, the members will be taught a lot of aspects involved in entrepreneurship, start-ups, business plannings while also attending to all their requirements and specifics to be understood by them. Related and up to date content will be made accessible to every interested student out there in addition to weekly community learning sessions on different stages involved in entrepreneurship and related domains.
