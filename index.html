ABOUT US




EDC SIES GST is home to many students who dream of start-up plans through a plethora of events and initiatives throughout the year,. We equip them with the necessary skills, promote creative and critical thinking amongst them, and provide them a large exposure to the information regarding the latest innovations in the market.
We are home to many students who have dreamt of a start-up plan and have helped them in execution of it from the scrap until it has got well settled in the market. EDC Cell is a proud council with start-ups like Festivus, Cosweb Technologies, PC Assembly Line Co., Orbit Hostings, Naspack in its credits.
We do much more than holding speaker sessions, workshops and competitions. We introduce Students to a whole new dimension which they never thought of venturing into.
